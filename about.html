Welcome To Crossline Community Church

Who we are

At Crossline Community Church, we are passionate about sharing the love of Christ, building strong relationships, and serving our community. Our mission is to lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ through worship, teaching, and outreach.

Our History

Founded in 2005, Crossline started as a small gathering of believers and has since grown into a vibrant community that welcomes people from all walks of life. With a heart for Southern California, we are committed to making an impact for Christ.
